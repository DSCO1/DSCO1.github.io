Poha Recipe

A Simple and Healthy Breakfast

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flattened rice (poha)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp ginger, grated
  • 1/2 cup boiled potatoes, cubed
  • A handful of peanuts
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp sugar (optional)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Steps

  • Step 1: Rinse poha (flattened rice) in a colander under cold water. Set aside to drain.
  • Step 2: Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds and cumin seeds, and let them splutter.
  • Step 3: Add chopped onion, green chili, and grated ginger. Sauté until onions become soft.
  • Step 4: Add turmeric powder, boiled potatoes, and peanuts. Stir and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  • Step 5: Add the rinsed poha to the pan. Add salt and sugar (optional), and mix gently.
  • Step 6: Cover and cook for 2-3 minutes on low heat. Turn off the heat.
  • Step 7: Add lemon juice and garnish with fresh coriander leaves. Serve hot.
