Masala Dosa Recipe

A Classic South Indian Dish

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • 2 boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • Curry leaves
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Oil (for cooking)

Steps

  • Step 1: Prepare the batter: Soak rice, dal, and fenugreek for 6-8 hours. Grind into a smooth batter. Ferment for 8-12 hours. Add salt.
  • Step 2: Make the masala: Saut√© mustard seeds, cumin, curry leaves, onions, and green chili. Add turmeric, grated ginger, and mashed potatoes. Season with salt and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  • Step 3: Cook the dosa: Heat a tawa, pour batter, spread thin. Drizzle oil and cook until golden. Add masala filling and fold.
  • Step 4: Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
