Jalebi Recipe

Crisp, Deep-Fried Spirals Soaked in Sugar Syrup

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • Water (as needed)
  • Oil or ghee for deep frying
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • A few saffron strands (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp lemon juice

Steps

  • Step 1: In a mixing bowl, combine all-purpose flour, cornflour, baking powder, and yogurt. Add water gradually to make a thick, smooth batter. Cover and ferment for 8-10 hours or overnight.
  • Step 2: Prepare the sugar syrup by boiling sugar, water, cardamom powder, saffron strands, and lemon juice. Simmer until the syrup is sticky but not too thick.
  • Step 3: Heat oil or ghee in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  • Step 4: Fill a piping bag or squeeze bottle with the fermented batter.
  • Step 5: Pipe the batter into hot oil in a spiral shape. Fry until golden and crisp on both sides.
  • Step 6: Remove the fried jalebis and immediately dip them into the warm sugar syrup for 2-3 minutes.
  • Step 7: Take out the jalebis and let excess syrup drip off. Serve warm.
