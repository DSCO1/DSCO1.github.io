Margherita Pizza Recipe

A Classic Pizza with Fresh Tomatoes, Basil, and Mozzarella

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup fresh mozzarella cheese (sliced)
  • 2 medium tomatoes (sliced)
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup tomato sauce (optional)

Steps

  • Step 1: Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C) and place a pizza stone or baking sheet inside to heat up.
  • Step 2: Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  • Step 3: If using, spread a thin layer of tomato sauce on the pizza dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  • Step 4: Arrange the sliced fresh mozzarella cheese evenly over the dough, followed by the sliced tomatoes.
  • Step 5: Drizzle olive oil over the pizza and sprinkle with oregano, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  • Step 6: Transfer the pizza to the preheated stone or baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  • Step 7: Remove the pizza from the oven and immediately top with fresh basil leaves.
  • Step 8: Slice and serve your delicious Margherita pizza while hot. Enjoy!
