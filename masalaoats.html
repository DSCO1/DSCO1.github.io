Masala Oats Recipe

A Healthy and Spicy Breakfast Option

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 small tomato (finely chopped)
  • 1 small carrot (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1-2 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger (grated)
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Steps

  • Step 1: Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  • Step 2: Add chopped onions, green chilies, and ginger. Sauté until onions are golden brown.
  • Step 3: Add tomatoes, carrots, and green peas. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  • Step 4: Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well.
  • Step 5: Add oats and mix everything thoroughly.
  • Step 6: Pour in 2 cups of water, stir well, and cook on low heat until the oats are soft and the mixture thickens.
  • Step 7: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.
