Veg Momos Recipe

A Delicious Tibetan Snack

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (for dough)
  • 1 tbsp oil (for dough)
  • 1/4 tsp salt (for dough)
  • 1 cup finely chopped mixed vegetables (cabbage, carrots, capsicum, beans)
  • 1 tbsp grated ginger
  • 1 tbsp garlic (finely chopped)
  • 1-2 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  • 1 tbsp oil (for stuffing)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Water (for kneading dough)
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Steps

  • Step 1: In a bowl, mix the flour, oil, and salt. Gradually add water to form a smooth dough. Cover and set aside for 30 minutes.
  • Step 2: Heat oil in a pan, sauté garlic, ginger, and green chilies until fragrant.
  • Step 3: Add the chopped vegetables and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes. Add soy sauce, black pepper, and salt. Stir well and remove from heat.
  • Step 4: Divide the dough into small balls. Roll out each ball into a small circle.
  • Step 5: Place a spoonful of the vegetable mixture in the center and fold the edges to seal, forming a momo.
  • Step 6: Steam the momos in a steamer for 10-12 minutes or until the dough becomes shiny and cooked.
  • Step 7: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with a dipping sauce.
