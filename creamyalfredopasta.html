Creamy Alfredo Pasta Recipe

A Rich and Creamy Pasta Dish with Garlic and Parmesan

Ingredients

  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta (or your preferred pasta)
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated mozzarella cheese
  • Salt to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Chopped parsley for garnish

Steps

  • Step 1: Cook the fettuccine pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside, reserving 1/2 cup of pasta water.
  • Step 2: Heat olive oil and butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  • Step 3: Add the heavy cream to the skillet and bring to a simmer. Cook for 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • Step 4: Gradually add the grated Parmesan and mozzarella cheeses to the sauce, stirring constantly until the sauce is smooth and creamy.
  • Step 5: Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste. If the sauce is too thick, add the reserved pasta water a little at a time until the desired consistency is reached.
  • Step 6: Add the cooked pasta to the skillet, tossing it in the creamy sauce until well-coated.
  • Step 7: Garnish with chopped parsley and serve immediately. Enjoy your creamy Alfredo pasta!
