Ragda Pattice Recipe

A Delicious Street Food

Ingredients

  • For Ragda (Peas Curry):
  • 1 cup dried white peas (soaked overnight)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1-2 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Water (for cooking)
  • For Pattice (Potato Patties):
  • 4 large potatoes (boiled and mashed)
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp chat masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Oil (for shallow frying)

Steps

  • Step 1: For the ragda, cook the soaked white peas in a pressure cooker with 3 cups of water until soft. Set aside.
  • Step 2: Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and sauté until they splutter. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  • Step 3: Add onions and sauté until golden. Add pureed tomatoes and cook until the oil separates from the masala.
  • Step 4: Add turmeric, garam masala, and salt. Mix well, then add the cooked peas along with some water to make a thick curry. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  • Step 5: For the pattice, mix mashed potatoes, bread crumbs, cumin powder, red chili powder, chat masala, turmeric powder, and salt in a bowl. Form into small patties.
  • Step 6: Heat oil in a pan and shallow fry the patties until golden and crispy on both sides. Drain on paper towels.
  • Step 7: To serve, place the hot pattice on a plate. Pour the ragda over them, and garnish with onions, coriander leaves, sev, and a squeeze of lemon juice.
