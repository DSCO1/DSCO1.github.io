Palak Paneer Recipe

A Creamy Spinach and Cottage Cheese Delight

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer (cottage cheese), cubed
  • 4 cups spinach (palak), washed and chopped
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 large tomato, pureed
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1-2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 cup cream (optional, for a creamy texture)
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Steps

  • Step 1: Blanch the spinach in boiling water for 2-3 minutes, then transfer to ice water. Grind into a smooth paste.
  • Step 2: Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Add cumin seeds and sauté for a few seconds.
  • Step 3: Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, cook for another 2 minutes.
  • Step 4: Add tomato puree and cook for 5-7 minutes, until the oil separates.
  • Step 5: Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Stir well.
  • Step 6: Add the spinach paste to the pan and mix well. Cook for 5-7 minutes on medium heat.
  • Step 7: Add the cubed paneer and mix gently. Cook for another 5 minutes.
  • Step 8: Add cream (if using) and cook for another 2 minutes. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Step 9: Serve hot with naan or rice.
