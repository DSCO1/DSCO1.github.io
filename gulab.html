Gulab Jamun Recipe

Soft and Sweet Milk Dumplings

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tbsp ghee (clarified butter)
  • 2-3 tbsp milk (for kneading)
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • Few drops of rose water
  • Oil or ghee for deep frying

Steps

  • Step 1: In a bowl, mix milk powder, all-purpose flour, and baking soda.
  • Step 2: Add ghee and mix until the mixture becomes crumbly.
  • Step 3: Gradually add milk and knead into a soft dough. Cover and let it rest for 10 minutes.
  • Step 4: Meanwhile, prepare sugar syrup by boiling sugar and water together until slightly sticky. Add cardamom powder and rose water. Keep warm.
  • Step 5: Divide the dough into small, smooth balls without cracks.
  • Step 6: Heat oil or ghee in a deep pan and fry the balls on low-medium heat until golden brown.
  • Step 7: Soak the fried balls in warm sugar syrup for at least 30 minutes before serving.
