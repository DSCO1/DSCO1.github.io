Aloo Paratha Recipe

A Popular North Indian Dish

Ingredients

  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • 3 boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Butter or ghee

Steps

  • Step 1: Knead soft dough using wheat flour and water.
  • Step 2: Mix mashed potatoes, cumin, chili powder, and salt for the stuffing.
  • Step 3: Roll out the dough, place the stuffing, and seal.
  • Step 4: Cook on a hot griddle with butter or ghee until golden brown.
