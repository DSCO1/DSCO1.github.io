Paneer Butter Masala Recipe

A North Indian Delight

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 large tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  • Step 1: Heat butter in a pan, sauté onions until translucent.
  • Step 2: Add turmeric, garam masala, and kasuri methi, then stir in tomato puree. Cook until oil separates.
  • Step 3: Add paneer cubes and cream, simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  • Step 4: Garnish with cream and serve with naan or roti.
