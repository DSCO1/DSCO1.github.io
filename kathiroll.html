Kathi Roll Recipe

A Delicious Indian Street Food

Ingredients

  • 4-5 paratha or tortilla wraps
  • 1 cup chicken or paneer (cubed)
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Mint chutney (optional)

Steps

  • Step 1: Marinate the chicken or paneer in yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, lemon juice, and salt for 30 minutes.
  • Step 2: Heat oil in a pan and sauté the marinated chicken or paneer until cooked and slightly browned.
  • Step 3: In the same pan, sauté the sliced onions and green chilies until they are soft and golden.
  • Step 4: Warm the paratha or tortilla wraps on a griddle or in a pan.
  • Step 5: Place the cooked chicken or paneer and sautéed onions in the center of the wrap.
  • Step 6: Drizzle with mint chutney (optional) and sprinkle fresh coriander leaves for garnish.
  • Step 7: Roll up the paratha or tortilla tightly and serve hot!
