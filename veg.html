Mixed Vegetable Curry Recipe

A Flavorful Indian Curry

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, beans, potatoes, cauliflower)
  • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 cup water (or as needed)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Steps

  • Step 1: Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and let them splutter.
  • Step 2: Add finely chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  • Step 3: Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another minute until fragrant.
  • Step 4: Add pureed tomatoes, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates from the masala.
  • Step 5: Add the mixed vegetables and sauté for a couple of minutes.
  • Step 6: Add water, cover, and cook for 15-20 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.
  • Step 7: Sprinkle garam masala and stir well. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with roti or rice.
