Kadi Pakora Recipe

A Popular North Indian Dish

Ingredients

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • Curry leaves, red chilies (for tempering)

Steps

  • Step 1: Mix besan, yogurt, and water to make a smooth batter.
  • Step 2: Cook on medium heat until thick, stirring constantly.
  • Step 3: Fry small besan pakoras and add to the kadi.
  • Step 4: Temper with cumin, red chilies, and curry leaves.
