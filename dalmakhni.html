Dal Makhani Recipe

A North Indian Favorite

Ingredients

  • 1 cup black urad dal (whole black lentils)
  • 1/4 cup kidney beans (rajma)
  • 2 large tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Steps

  • Step 1: Soak the urad dal and rajma overnight, then pressure cook until soft.
  • Step 2: Heat oil in a pan. Add garlic paste and sauté until fragrant.
  • Step 3: Add onions, cook until golden, then add turmeric, red chili powder, and salt.
  • Step 4: Add tomato puree and cook until the oil separates.
  • Step 5: Stir in the cooked lentils. Simmer for 20-25 minutes.
  • Step 6: Add butter and cream, simmer for another 5 minutes.
  • Step 7: Garnish with coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.
