Caesar Salad Recipe

A Classic Salad with Crisp Romaine Lettuce, Parmesan, and Creamy Dressing

Ingredients

  • 4 cups romaine lettuce (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup croutons
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp grated Parmesan cheese (for dressing)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  • Step 1: In a large bowl, combine the chopped romaine lettuce and croutons.
  • Step 2: In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, minced garlic, mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  • Step 3: Pour the dressing over the lettuce and croutons. Toss gently to coat the salad evenly.
  • Step 4: Sprinkle additional grated Parmesan cheese on top for garnish, if desired.
  • Step 5: Serve immediately and enjoy your fresh and creamy Caesar salad!
