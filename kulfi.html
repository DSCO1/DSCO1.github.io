Kulfi Recipe

Traditional Indian Ice Cream Made from Thickened Milk

Ingredients

  • 2 cups full cream milk
  • 1 cup condensed milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1-2 strands saffron (optional)
  • 1/4 cup chopped pistachios (optional)
  • 1/4 cup chopped almonds (optional)
  • 1 tbsp rose water (optional)
  • Ice cream molds or small containers

Steps

  • Step 1: In a heavy-bottomed pan, boil the full cream milk on medium heat while stirring constantly to avoid burning.
  • Step 2: Once the milk reduces to about half its volume, add the condensed milk and sugar. Stir well to dissolve the sugar.
  • Step 3: Add cardamom powder and saffron (if using). Continue to simmer the mixture, stirring occasionally, until it thickens further. Remove from heat and let it cool for a bit.
  • Step 4: Add rose water (optional) and mix in chopped pistachios and almonds for added flavor and texture.
  • Step 5: Pour the mixture into ice cream molds or small containers and cover with plastic wrap or lids.
  • Step 6: Freeze the kulfi for 6-8 hours or overnight until completely set.
  • Step 7: To serve, run warm water over the outside of the molds to release the kulfi. Serve chilled and garnish with additional pistachios or almonds if desired.
