Ladoo Recipe

Delicious Indian Sweet Balls Made with Flour, Sugar, and Ghee

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chickpea flour (besan)
  • 1/2 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1/4 cup chopped cashews
  • 1/4 cup chopped almonds
  • 1/4 cup golden raisins
  • A pinch of saffron strands (optional)
  • 1 tbsp milk (for binding, if needed)

Steps

  • Step 1: In a heavy-bottomed pan, heat ghee over low heat. Add the chickpea flour and roast it gently, stirring continuously until the flour turns golden brown and releases a nutty aroma.
  • Step 2: Add chopped cashews, almonds, and raisins to the pan and sauté them with the flour for 2-3 minutes until they are lightly roasted.
  • Step 3: Sprinkle in the cardamom powder and saffron strands (if using), mixing everything well.
  • Step 4: Gradually add the powdered sugar to the mixture, stirring until the sugar completely melts and incorporates with the flour mixture.
  • Step 5: If the mixture is too dry, add a little milk to help bind it together. Continue stirring until the mixture is smooth and has a dough-like consistency.
  • Step 6: Allow the mixture to cool slightly. Once it's comfortable to handle, grease your hands with a little ghee and shape the mixture into small round balls (laddoos).
  • Step 7: Let the ladoos cool completely and set. Serve and enjoy!
