Palak Pakora Recipe

Crispy Spinach Fritters

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh spinach leaves (palak), chopped
  • 1 cup chickpea flour (besan)
  • 1/4 cup rice flour
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp ajwain (carom seeds)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Water, as needed
  • Oil for deep frying

Steps

  • Step 1: In a large mixing bowl, combine chopped spinach, chickpea flour, rice flour, onion, green chilies, ginger paste, cumin, ajwain, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt.
  • Step 2: Add a little water and mix everything well to form a thick batter. The mixture should be sticky but not too runny.
  • Step 3: Heat oil in a deep pan or kadai for frying.
  • Step 4: Once the oil is hot, take small portions of the batter and drop them carefully into the hot oil. Fry in batches to avoid overcrowding.
  • Step 5: Fry the pakoras until golden and crispy, turning occasionally to ensure even cooking.
  • Step 6: Remove the pakoras from the oil and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  • Step 7: Serve the hot, crispy palak pakoras with green chutney or tamarind chutney.
