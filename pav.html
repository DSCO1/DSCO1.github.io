Pav Bhaji Recipe

A Famous Maharashtrian Street Food

Ingredients

  • 4 boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp pav bhaji masala
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 pav buns

Steps

  • Step 1: Heat butter, sauté onions until golden, and add tomatoes. Cook until soft.
  • Step 2: Add vegetables, pav bhaji masala, and mash. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  • Step 3: Toast pav buns with butter and serve with the bhaji.
