Upma Recipe

A Popular South Indian Breakfast

Ingredients

  • 1 cup semolina (rava)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp urad dal (optional)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • A handful of curry leaves
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Steps

  • Step 1: Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, urad dal, and curry leaves. Sauté for a minute.
  • Step 2: Add chopped onions and green chilies, sauté until onions turn golden brown.
  • Step 3: Add turmeric powder and salt. Then add water and bring it to a boil.
  • Step 4: Gradually add semolina (rava) while stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  • Step 5: Cook for 5-7 minutes until the water is absorbed and the mixture thickens.
  • Step 6: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.
