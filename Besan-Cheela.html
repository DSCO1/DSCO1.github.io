Besan Chilla Recipe

A Healthy and Tasty Indian Pancake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped vegetables (onion, tomato, bell pepper, spinach, etc.)
  • 1-2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp ginger, grated
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed to make the batter
  • Oil for cooking
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Steps

  • Step 1: In a mixing bowl, add gram flour (besan), chopped vegetables, green chilies, cumin seeds, turmeric powder, red chili powder, grated ginger, and salt.
  • Step 2: Gradually add water to form a smooth batter, similar to pancake batter consistency.
  • Step 3: Heat a non-stick pan or griddle and lightly grease with oil.
  • Step 4: Pour a ladle of the batter onto the pan and spread it into a round shape, similar to a pancake.
  • Step 5: Cook on medium heat for 2-3 minutes until the edges turn golden brown. Flip it over and cook the other side for another 2 minutes.
  • Step 6: Once cooked, remove from the pan and repeat with the remaining batter.
  • Step 7: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with chutney or yogurt.
