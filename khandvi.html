Khandvi Recipe

A Traditional Gujarati Snack

Ingredients

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1 tbsp ginger paste
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 1 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1-2 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 1/4 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)
  • Coconut flakes (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  • Step 1: Mix gram flour, water, ginger paste, turmeric powder, and salt in a pan. Stir well to avoid lumps.
  • Step 2: Cook the mixture on low flame, stirring continuously until it thickens and starts to leave the sides of the pan.
  • Step 3: Once the mixture is thick, spread it evenly on a greased surface (like a countertop) to cool.
  • Step 4: When the mixture has cooled slightly, cut it into long strips.
  • Step 5: Roll each strip into a tight cylinder, creating the khandvi rolls.
  • Step 6: In a pan, heat ghee or oil, and add mustard seeds, sesame seeds, and asafoetida. Let them splutter.
  • Step 7: Add the chopped green chilies and pour the tempering over the rolled khandvi.
  • Step 8: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and optional coconut flakes.
  • Step 9: Serve khandvi warm or at room temperature as a snack or appetizer.
