Misal Pav Recipe

A Spicy Maharashtrian Street Food Delight

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sprouted moth beans (matki)
  • 2 onions (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (finely chopped)
  • 2 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp misal masala (optional)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)
  • 1 cup sev (crispy chickpea noodles)
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions (for garnish)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Pav (Indian bread rolls)
  • Lemon wedges (for serving)

Steps

  • Step 1: Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  • Step 2: Add chopped onions, green chilies, and ginger-garlic paste. Sauté until onions are golden brown.
  • Step 3: Add tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and misal masala. Cook until oil separates.
  • Step 4: Add sprouted moth beans and mix well. Cook for 5-7 minutes.
  • Step 5: Pour in water as needed to adjust the consistency and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  • Step 6: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Step 7: To serve, place some misal in a bowl, top with chopped onions, sev, and a lemon wedge. Serve with buttered pav on the side.
