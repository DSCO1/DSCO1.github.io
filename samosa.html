Samosa Recipe

A Classic Indian Snack

Ingredients

  • 2 cups boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1/2 cup boiled peas
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1-2 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger (grated)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh coriander leaves
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 12 samosa wrappers or dough for making wrappers
  • Oil (for deep frying)

Steps

  • Step 1: Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds, mustard seeds, green chilies, and grated ginger. Sauté for a minute.
  • Step 2: Add turmeric powder, garam masala, coriander powder, red chili powder, and salt. Stir well to combine.
  • Step 3: Add boiled mashed potatoes and peas to the pan. Mix everything well and cook for 5 minutes.
  • Step 4: Stir in lemon juice and fresh coriander leaves. Let the filling cool.
  • Step 5: Take the samosa wrappers or prepared dough, shape them into cones, and fill them with the prepared filling.
  • Step 6: Seal the edges of the cones to form samosas. Make sure they are tightly sealed.
  • Step 7: Heat oil in a deep pan for frying. Fry the samosas in batches until golden brown and crispy.
  • Step 8: Remove the samosas and drain excess oil on paper towels. Serve hot with chutney or sauce.
