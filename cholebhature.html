Chole Bhature Recipe

A Punjabi Classic

Ingredients for Chole

  • 2 cups chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 2 large onions (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp chole masala
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp oil

Ingredients for Bhature

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

Chole:

  • Step 1: Pressure cook chickpeas with salt until soft.
  • Step 2: Heat oil, saut√© cumin seeds, onions, and ginger-garlic paste until golden.
  • Step 3: Add tomato puree, turmeric, garam masala, and chole masala. Cook until fragrant.
  • Step 4: Mix in chickpeas and simmer for 15 minutes.

Bhature:

  • Step 1: Knead a soft dough using flour, yogurt, salt, and baking soda.
  • Step 2: Let the dough rest for 2 hours.
  • Step 3: Roll out and deep-fry to golden perfection.
  • Step 4: Serve chole with hot bhature.
