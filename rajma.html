Rajma Recipe

A North Indian Comfort Food

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rajma (kidney beans)
  • 2 large tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp coriander powder
  • 1 tbsp cumin seeds
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Water (for cooking)

Steps

  • Step 1: Soak the rajma overnight or for at least 8 hours. Drain the water and set aside.
  • Step 2: In a pressure cooker, add soaked rajma with 3 cups of water. Cook for 6-7 whistles or until soft and tender.
  • Step 3: Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and let them splutter. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  • Step 4: Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for another minute. Then add turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, coriander powder, and salt.
  • Step 5: Stir in the pureed tomatoes and cook until the oil separates from the masala.
  • Step 6: Add the cooked rajma along with some water to achieve desired consistency. Simmer the curry for 15-20 minutes.
  • Step 7: Add lemon juice and garnish with fresh coriander leaves. Serve hot with rice or roti.
