Kheer Recipe

Creamy Rice Pudding Cooked with Milk, Sugar, and Flavored with Cardamom and Saffron

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup basmati rice (washed and soaked for 30 minutes)
  • 1 liter whole milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • A few saffron strands (soaked in warm milk)
  • 10-12 almonds (sliced)
  • 10-12 cashews (chopped)
  • 10-12 pistachios (sliced)
  • 10-12 raisins

Steps

  • Step 1: In a heavy-bottomed pan, bring the milk to a boil, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.
  • Step 2: Add the soaked and drained basmati rice. Cook on low heat, stirring frequently, until the rice is fully cooked and the milk thickens.
  • Step 3: Add sugar and continue cooking until it dissolves completely.
  • Step 4: Mix in cardamom powder and saffron-infused milk. Stir well.
  • Step 5: In a small pan, lightly roast the almonds, cashews, pistachios, and raisins in ghee until golden brown.
  • Step 6: Add the roasted nuts and raisins to the kheer. Mix well and cook for another 5-7 minutes.
  • Step 7: Serve warm or chilled, garnished with extra nuts and saffron strands.
