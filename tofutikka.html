Tofu Tikka Recipe

A Vegan Delight

Ingredients

  • 250g tofu (pressed and cut into cubes)
  • 1/2 cup thick yogurt (dairy-free if vegan)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)
  • Wooden skewers (optional, for grilling)

Steps

  • Step 1: Press tofu to remove excess water, then cut into cubes.
  • Step 2: In a mixing bowl, combine yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, olive oil, cumin powder, coriander powder, garam masala, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt to make the marinade.
  • Step 3: Add tofu cubes to the marinade and coat evenly. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  • Step 4: Preheat the grill or oven to 180°C (350°F). If using skewers, thread the tofu cubes onto the skewers.
  • Step 5: Grill or bake the tofu for 15-20 minutes, until golden and slightly charred on the edges.
  • Step 6: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.
