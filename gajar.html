Gajar Halwa Recipe

A Sweet Indian Carrot Dessert

Ingredients

  • 4-5 medium-sized carrots (grated)
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • 10-12 cashews (chopped)
  • 10-12 almonds (chopped)
  • 10-12 raisins
  • 1 tbsp chopped pistachios (optional)
  • 1/4 cup khoya or mawa (optional for richer taste)

Steps

  • Step 1: Heat ghee in a pan and sauté the grated carrots for 5 minutes until they soften.
  • Step 2: Add milk to the pan and cook the carrots in the milk on low flame until the milk evaporates.
  • Step 3: Once the milk is absorbed, add sugar and cardamom powder. Stir well.
  • Step 4: Cook the mixture on low flame until it thickens and the ghee starts to separate.
  • Step 5: Add khoya (if using), and continue to cook for a few more minutes until the halwa becomes rich and creamy.
  • Step 6: In a separate pan, heat a little ghee and fry the chopped cashews, almonds, and raisins until golden brown. Add these to the halwa and mix well.
  • Step 7: Garnish with chopped pistachios and serve hot or warm.
