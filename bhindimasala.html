Bhindi Masala Recipe

A Flavorful Indian Stir-Fry

Ingredients

  • 500 grams bhindi (okra), sliced
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  • Step 1: Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  • Step 2: Add ginger-garlic paste and saut√© until fragrant.
  • Step 3: Add onions and cook until they turn golden brown.
  • Step 4: Add tomatoes and cook until soft. Stir in turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, coriander powder, and salt.
  • Step 5: Add the sliced bhindi (okra) and cook on medium heat until tender and lightly browned, stirring occasionally.
  • Step 6: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with roti or rice.
