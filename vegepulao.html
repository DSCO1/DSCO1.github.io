Vegetable Pulao Recipe

A Flavorful Rice Dish with Vegetables

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, beans, potatoes)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 cloves
  • 1-inch cinnamon stick
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Steps

  • Step 1: Wash the rice and soak it for 20 minutes. Drain the water and set aside.
  • Step 2: Heat oil or ghee in a pan, add cumin seeds, bay leaf, cloves, and cinnamon stick. Sauté for a minute.
  • Step 3: Add the chopped onion and sauté until golden brown. Then add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, cook for a minute.
  • Step 4: Add the mixed vegetables and sauté for 2-3 minutes.
  • Step 5: Add turmeric powder, salt, and the soaked rice. Stir gently to mix.
  • Step 6: Add water and bring it to a boil. Reduce the heat, cover, and cook for 10-12 minutes until the rice is fully cooked.
  • Step 7: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with raita or curry.
