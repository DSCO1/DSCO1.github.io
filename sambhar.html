Sambhar Recipe

A Traditional Tamil Nadu Dish

Ingredients

  • 1 cup toor dal (split pigeon peas)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (okra, carrots, drumsticks)
  • 2 tbsp sambhar powder
  • Tamarind pulp (to taste)
  • Mustard seeds, curry leaves (for tempering)

Steps

  • Step 1: Pressure cook toor dal with turmeric until soft.
  • Step 2: Cook vegetables with sambhar powder and tamarind pulp.
  • Step 3: Add cooked dal, simmer for 10 minutes.
  • Step 4: Temper with mustard seeds and curry leaves.
