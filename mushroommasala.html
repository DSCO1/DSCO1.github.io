Mushroom Masala Recipe

A Spicy, Flavorful Dish

Ingredients

  • 250g mushrooms (sliced)
  • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Steps

  • Step 1: Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and sauté until fragrant.
  • Step 2: Add onions and cook until golden brown. Stir in ginger garlic paste and cook for another minute.
  • Step 3: Add the pureed tomatoes, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates.
  • Step 4: Add sliced mushrooms to the pan and cook for 5-6 minutes until they release water and become soft.
  • Step 5: Stir in garam masala powder and cook for another 2 minutes.
  • Step 6: Add cream and mix well. Simmer for 5 minutes on low heat.
  • Step 7: Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with roti or rice.
