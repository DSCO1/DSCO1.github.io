Bhel Puri Recipe

A Popular Indian Street Food Snack

Ingredients

  • 2 cups puffed rice (murmura)
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 small tomato (finely chopped)
  • 1 boiled potato (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup boiled peanuts
  • 1/4 cup sev (crispy chickpea noodles)
  • 2-3 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp tamarind chutney
  • 2 tbsp green chutney (coriander-mint)
  • 1 tsp chaat masala
  • 1/2 tsp roasted cumin powder
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice

Steps

  • Step 1: In a large mixing bowl, combine puffed rice, chopped onions, tomatoes, boiled potatoes, and peanuts.
  • Step 2: Add green chilies, tamarind chutney, green chutney, chaat masala, roasted cumin powder, and salt.
  • Step 3: Mix everything well, ensuring the chutneys coat all the ingredients.
  • Step 4: Add lemon juice and mix gently.
  • Step 5: Just before serving, sprinkle sev and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Step 6: Serve immediately for the best crunch and flavor.
