Mysore Pak Recipe

A Delicious Indian Sweet

Ingredients

  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder (optional)

Steps

  • Step 1: Heat ghee in a pan on medium heat. Add besan and roast it for 5-7 minutes, stirring constantly until the raw smell disappears.
  • Step 2: In a separate pan, heat water and sugar to make sugar syrup. Stir until the sugar dissolves and the syrup reaches a single thread consistency.
  • Step 3: Slowly add the sugar syrup to the roasted besan while stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  • Step 4: Continue cooking the mixture on low flame, stirring until it starts to thicken and leave the sides of the pan.
  • Step 5: Add cardamom powder (optional) and stir well. Transfer the mixture to a greased plate or tray.
  • Step 6: Let it cool for 10-15 minutes, then cut into squares or desired shapes.
  • Step 7: Serve and enjoy the delicious Mysore Pak!
