Veg Hakka Noodles Recipe

A Chinese-Inspired Delight

Ingredients

  • 200 grams Hakka noodles
  • 1/2 cup cabbage (shredded)
  • 1/4 cup carrots (julienned)
  • 1/4 cup capsicum (bell peppers, julienned)
  • 1/4 cup spring onions (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup beansprouts (optional)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tbsp green chili sauce
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp oil (for stir-frying)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil (optional, for extra flavor)
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Steps

  • Step 1: Cook the Hakka noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and rinse with cold water to prevent them from sticking together. Set aside.
  • Step 2: Heat oil in a large pan or wok. Add the chopped spring onions and sauté for a minute.
  • Step 3: Add the shredded cabbage, carrots, and capsicum to the pan. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are tender yet crunchy.
  • Step 4: Add soy sauce, vinegar, green chili sauce, black pepper, and sugar to the vegetables. Stir well to coat the veggies with the sauces.
  • Step 5: Add the cooked noodles to the pan and toss everything together for 2-3 minutes. Add salt to taste.
  • Step 6: Drizzle sesame oil over the noodles for extra flavor, if desired. Toss well to combine.
  • Step 7: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot. Enjoy with your favorite dipping sauce!
