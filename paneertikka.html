Spicy Paneer Tikka Masala Recipe

A Rich and Flavorful Indian Dish

Ingredients

  • 250 grams paneer (cubed)
  • 1/2 cup thick yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp corn flour
  • 1 tbsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala (for the gravy)
  • 1/4 cup water

Steps

  • Step 1: In a mixing bowl, combine yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, lemon juice, corn flour, kasuri methi, oil, and salt. Mix well to form a smooth marinade.
  • Step 2: Add the paneer cubes to the marinade and coat them evenly. Cover and let it marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes.
  • Step 3: Heat oil in a pan and sauté the chopped onions until golden brown. Add the pureed tomatoes, cumin seeds, and butter. Cook for 5-7 minutes until the oil separates.
  • Step 4: Add garam masala, salt, and water to the gravy. Stir well and simmer for another 5 minutes to combine the flavors.
  • Step 5: While the gravy is simmering, grill the marinated paneer cubes on a grill pan or in the oven until slightly charred (10-15 minutes at 200°C or 392°F).
  • Step 6: Once the paneer is done, add it to the gravy and mix gently. Let it cook for another 5-10 minutes, allowing the paneer to absorb the flavors.
  • Step 7: Add cream to the gravy, stir well, and let it simmer for another 2 minutes.
  • Step 8: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan, roti, or rice. Enjoy your Spicy Paneer Tikka Masala!
