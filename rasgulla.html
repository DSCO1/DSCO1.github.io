Rasgulla Recipe

Soft and Spongy Cheese Dumplings Soaked in Light Sugar Syrup

Ingredients

  • 1 liter full cream milk
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice (or vinegar)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 4 cups water
  • 2-3 cardamom pods (crushed, optional)
  • Few drops of rose water (optional)

Steps

  • Step 1: Boil the milk in a heavy-bottomed pan. Once it boils, lower the heat and slowly add lemon juice while stirring until the milk curdles.
  • Step 2: Turn off the heat and let it sit for 5 minutes. Strain the curdled milk through a muslin cloth to separate the chenna (cheese).
  • Step 3: Rinse the chenna under cold water to remove the lemon flavor. Squeeze out excess water gently.
  • Step 4: Knead the chenna for 8-10 minutes until it becomes smooth and soft. Make small balls with no cracks.
  • Step 5: In a large pan, boil water with sugar and crushed cardamom pods to make light syrup.
  • Step 6: Gently drop the chenna balls into the boiling syrup, cover, and cook on medium heat for 15-20 minutes.
  • Step 7: Turn off the heat and let the rasgullas cool in the syrup. Add rose water if using.
  • Step 8: Serve chilled or at room temperature.
