Tandoori Mushroom Recipe

A Smoky and Spicy Delight

Ingredients

  • 250g mushrooms (whole, cleaned)
  • 1/2 cup thick yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Fresh coriander (for garnish)
  • Charcoal for smoky flavor (optional)

Steps

  • Step 1: Clean the mushrooms and remove the stems. Set aside.
  • Step 2: In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, cumin powder, lemon juice, oil, and salt to make the marinade.
  • Step 3: Coat the mushrooms in the marinade, cover, and refrigerate for 30 minutes to 1 hour.
  • Step 4: Preheat the grill or oven to 200°C (392°F). If using a grill, skewer the mushrooms.
  • Step 5: Grill the mushrooms for 15-20 minutes or until they are tender and lightly charred.
  • Step 6: If using charcoal, place a small piece of hot coal in a bowl, add a few drops of oil, and place it in the cooked mushrooms for a smoky flavor.
  • Step 7: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with mint chutney or naan.
