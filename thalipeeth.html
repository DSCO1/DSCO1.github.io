Thalipeeth Recipe

A Savory Multigrain Flatbread

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1/4 cup chickpea flour (besan)
  • 1/4 cup rice flour
  • 1/4 cup jowar flour (sorghum flour)
  • 1/4 cup bajra flour (pearl millet flour)
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 2-3 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp carom seeds (ajwain)
  • Fresh coriander leaves (finely chopped)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Water (for kneading)
  • Oil or ghee (for cooking)

Steps

  • Step 1: In a large mixing bowl, combine all the flours, chopped onions, green chilies, ginger-garlic paste, spices, and salt.
  • Step 2: Add water little by little and knead the mixture into a smooth, slightly stiff dough.
  • Step 3: Divide the dough into small portions and roll each portion into a ball.
  • Step 4: Flatten each dough ball into a thick circle using your fingers or a rolling pin, making small indentations on the surface using your fingertips.
  • Step 5: Heat a griddle or tawa and grease it with a little oil or ghee.
  • Step 6: Place the rolled dough on the tawa and cook on medium heat. Flip and cook the other side until golden brown and crisp.
  • Step 7: Repeat for the remaining dough portions.
  • Step 8: Serve the thalipeeth hot with yogurt or chutney of your choice.
