Veggie Stir Fry Recipe

A Quick and Healthy Stir Fry of Colorful Vegetables

Ingredients

  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers (sliced, any color)
  • 1/2 cup carrots (julienned)
  • 1/2 cup snap peas
  • 1 small onion (sliced)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp fresh ginger (minced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds (for garnish)
  • Fresh cilantro (for garnish)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  • Step 1: Heat olive oil and sesame oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  • Step 2: Add the sliced onion, garlic, and ginger. Stir-fry for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  • Step 3: Add the broccoli, bell peppers, carrots, and snap peas to the pan. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  • Step 4: Drizzle soy sauce, rice vinegar, and honey (optional) over the vegetables. Stir well to coat evenly.
  • Step 5: Season with salt and pepper to taste, and continue stir-frying for another 1-2 minutes.
  • Step 6: Remove from heat and garnish with sesame seeds and fresh cilantro.
  • Step 7: Serve immediately as a main dish or side dish. Enjoy your healthy and delicious veggie stir fry!
