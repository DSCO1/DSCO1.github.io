Raita Recipe

A Refreshing Yogurt Side Dish

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/4 cup cucumber (grated or finely chopped)
  • 1/4 cup onion (finely chopped)
  • 1-2 green chilies (finely chopped, optional)
  • 1 tbsp fresh coriander (chopped)
  • 1/4 tsp roasted cumin powder
  • 1/4 tsp black salt
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 1/4 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp sugar (optional, for balance)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Steps

  • Step 1: In a bowl, whisk the yogurt until smooth and creamy.
  • Step 2: Add grated cucumber, chopped onions, green chilies (if using), and fresh coriander leaves to the yogurt.
  • Step 3: Sprinkle roasted cumin powder, black salt, red chili powder, and salt to taste. Add sugar if you like a slight sweetness.
  • Step 4: Add lemon juice and mix everything well to combine.
  • Step 5: Chill the raita in the refrigerator for 15-20 minutes for a refreshing taste.
  • Step 6: Garnish with a sprinkle of cumin powder and coriander leaves before serving.
  • Step 7: Serve chilled as a side dish with biryani, pulao, or any main course.
