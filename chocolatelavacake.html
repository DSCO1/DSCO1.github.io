Chocolate Lava Cake Recipe

A Decadent Dessert with a Melting Center

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 6 oz semi-sweet chocolate (chopped)
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • Pinch of salt
  • Butter (for greasing ramekins)
  • Cocoa powder (for dusting ramekins)
  • Vanilla ice cream or whipped cream (for serving)

Steps

  • Step 1: Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). Grease 4 ramekins with butter and dust with cocoa powder to prevent sticking.
  • Step 2: In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter and chocolate together in 30-second intervals, stirring until smooth. Let it cool slightly.
  • Step 3: In a separate bowl, whisk together powdered sugar, eggs, egg yolks, and vanilla extract until well combined and smooth.
  • Step 4: Add the melted chocolate mixture to the egg mixture and stir until fully incorporated.
  • Step 5: Sift in the flour and a pinch of salt. Gently fold the mixture until smooth and combined.
  • Step 6: Pour the batter evenly into the prepared ramekins, filling them about 3/4 full.
  • Step 7: Bake for 12-14 minutes, or until the edges are set but the center is still slightly soft (a toothpick inserted should come out mostly clean).
  • Step 8: Let the cakes cool for 1-2 minutes before carefully inverting them onto plates.
  • Step 9: Serve the chocolate lava cakes immediately with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream. Enjoy!
