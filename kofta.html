Malai Kofta Recipe

A Rich and Creamy Delight

Ingredients

  • 2 cups boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1/2 cup paneer (cottage cheese), grated
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 1/4 tsp garam masala
  • 1/4 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Oil (for frying)
  • For Gravy:
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 large tomato (pureed)
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Steps

  • Step 1: In a mixing bowl, combine boiled mashed potatoes, grated paneer, cornflour, garam masala, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well and form into small round balls.
  • Step 2: Heat oil in a pan and deep fry the koftas until golden brown. Remove and set aside on paper towels to drain excess oil.
  • Step 3: For the gravy, heat oil in a pan. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  • Step 4: Add ginger garlic paste and sauté for a minute, then add the pureed tomatoes. Cook until the oil separates from the mixture.
  • Step 5: Add garam masala, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  • Step 6: Add fresh cream and mix well. Simmer the gravy for 5-7 minutes on low heat.
  • Step 7: Gently add the fried koftas into the gravy and simmer for another 5 minutes, allowing them to soak up the flavors.
  • Step 8: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.
