Baigan Bharta Recipe

A North Indian Classic

Ingredients

  • 1 large eggplant (roasted and peeled)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 green chilies (chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Steps

  • Step 1: Roast the eggplant on an open flame until charred, peel the skin, and mash.
  • Step 2: Heat oil, add cumin seeds, onion, green chilies, and ginger-garlic paste. Cook until golden.
  • Step 3: Add tomatoes, cook until soft, and mix in the mashed eggplant.
  • Step 4: Cook for 5-7 minutes, garnish with coriander, and serve with roti.
